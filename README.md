## Project overview 
**Business Problem**: Megaline, a telecommunications operator, needs to determine which of their two prepaid plans (Surf vs Ultimate) generates more revenue to optimize their advertising budget allocation.
**My Role**: Data Analyst conducting comprehensive statistical analysis on customer behavior patterns and revenue generation across different plans and geographic regions.
**Dataset**: 500 customers' usage data from 2018 including calls, SMS, internet usage, and demographic information.

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. **Which plan generates higher average revenue?** (Surf vs Ultimate)
2. **Do geographic regions impact revenue?** (NY-NJ vs Other regions)
3. **What are the usage patterns for each customer segment?**
4. **How can Megaline optimize their marketing spend?**
